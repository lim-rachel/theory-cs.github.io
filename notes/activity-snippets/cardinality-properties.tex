{\bf Properties of cardinality}
\begin{align*}
&\forall A ~ (~  |A| = |A| ~)\\
&\forall A ~ \forall B ~(~ |A| = |B|  ~\to ~ |B| = |A|~)\\
&\forall A ~ \forall B ~ \forall C~ (~ (|A| = |B| ~\wedge~ |B| = |C|) ~\to ~ |A| = |C|~)
\end{align*}

{\it Extra practice with proofs:} Use the definitions of bijections to prove these properties.
