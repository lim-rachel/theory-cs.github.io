For each of the following compound propositions, determine
if it is in DNF, CNF, both, or neither.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item $(x \lor y \lor z) \land (x \land \lnot y \land z)$
    \item $\lnot (x \land y \land z) \land \lnot (\lnot x \land y \land \lnot z)$
\end{enumerate}
